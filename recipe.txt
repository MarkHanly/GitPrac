How to make the perfect cup of tea.

Ingredients:
Teabag x1
Milk to taste
Biscuits

Preparation:
Boil the kettle
Wash the cup (tea tastes best in a clean cup)

Steps:
1. Scald the cup with boiling water;
2. Put a teabag in the cup and pour water directly over;
3. Allow to brew for 3-4 minutes;
4. Using a teaspoon, squeeze the teabag firmly in the cup before removing;
5. Add milk to taste;
6. Serve with a biscuit.
